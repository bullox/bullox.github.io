So I've got this [bullo.cc](http://bullo.cc) thing, which has some links to my various places on the web and my CV and a nice little portfolio of projects I like. That's all nice. But it's also got a blog section -- what am I actually trying to achieve with that?

## For myself

I find that my brain is generally all over the place -- I do a lot of things across a number of different sectors, and the only place where there is currently a comprehensive picture of what those things are and how they fit together is in my head. My head is quite full, and I'm increasingly finding that things slip out of it or stay all jumbled up. 

I use a to-do list manager to help stop things slipping, but I also need a reason to take a step back and understand the shape of things, and what I want to focus on to achieve my big goals (and, as a matter of fact, what those big goals are!).
