I’m an engineer, teacher, communicator and maker --- rarely in that order. I currently spend a large amount of my time in the [Faculty of Engineering](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/engineering/) at the [University of Bristol](http://www.bristol.ac.uk/).

## Things I do

Lots of stuff! I think it's called a '[portfolio career](http://www.theguardian.com/education/2014/nov/27/could-a-portfolio-career-be-for-you)' by the cool kids. I quite like it.

Some things I do/have done:

* Teach Engineers to engineer, and to be human, on the [Aerospace programmes](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/aerospace/) at Bristol.
* Lead training and development across Europe/Africa-wide [WildDrone](http://wilddrone.eu).
* Worry about our impact on the world ([article from one of my PhD students](https://theconversation.com/why-the-aviation-industry-must-look-beyond-carbon-to-get-serious-about-climate-change-186947); [paper](https://www.mdpi.com/2226-4310/9/7/355)).
* Build [interactive art](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/news/2018/october/gromit-returns.html) that makes people happy.
* Try to share [my enthusiasm for making things happen, and my struggles along the way](https://physicsworld.com/a/embracing-a-life-of-variables/).
